What coaching is

Coaching is future-focused — it's about where you want to go and what's in the way. You choose the direction; I lead from behind. My job isn't to solve your problems. It's to help you develop the capability to solve them yourself.

That means more than asking good questions — though that matters. It also means sharing honest observations, challenging beliefs that aren't serving you, naming the things in the room that usually go unsaid, and having the courage to go somewhere uncomfortable when that's what's needed.

What my clients share: they're capable, they're motivated, and something is in the way that they can't navigate without help. Maybe you share that too?

What coaching looks like with me

Usually: 3–6 months  ·  bi-weekly, 60-min sessions

01

What's the challenge?

We get clear on what brought you here — the friction, the ceiling, the thing that's not working. And we build a picture of where you genuinely want to go: not just the surface goal, but the inspired future that will motivate you to push through.

02

Where are you now?

We map your current reality — strengths, blind spots, context, patterns. Sometimes this is quick. Sometimes we bring in outside perspective. Either way, you end up with an honest starting point.

03

Let's go!

How do we get from where you are to where you want to be? We build the plan and you work it — session by session, obstacle by obstacle — adjusting as you grow until you're through the fog and running smooth.
